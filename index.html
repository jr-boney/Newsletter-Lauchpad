Recap: Launchpad Kerala 2023

The annual job fair, hosted by IEEE Kerala Section and GTech MuLearn, and sponsored by the Kerala Knowledge Economy Mission (KKEM) and the National Institute of Electronics & Information Technology (NIELIT), was a significant milestone for professionals and job seekers.

This event brought together a diverse array of talented individuals, cutting-edge technology companies, and industry experts, creating an environment ripe with opportunities and innovation. Job seekers had the chance to interact with renowned organizations, gain valuable insights, and explore potential career paths.

Important Update: Online Aptitude and Mock Test

Dear Participant,

We hope this message finds you well. Launchpad Kerala is progressing into an exciting phase, and we invite you to the next step in our selection process.

Online Aptitude Test:

The online aptitude test is scheduled for July 4th. Participation is mandatory for all candidates aiming to proceed to the interview round. Your performance in this test, along with your technical challenge results, will determine your position in the final rank list. Interviews will be conducted in Trivandrum and Kochi based on this ranking.

Mock Test:

To help you prepare effectively, we will be conducting a mock test on July 3rd. Further details will be shared via a separate email soon.

Eligibility:

Only candidates who have completed at least one task in the Technical Challenge by July 1st will be eligible to participate in the aptitude test. If you haven't started the technical challenge yet, we encourage you to begin as soon as possible. Detailed instructions are available here.

Take Action:

The deadline to start your technical challenge is July 1st. This is a critical step towards launching your career with Launchpad Kerala. Don't miss this opportunity to showcase your skills and potential.

We look forward to your participation and wish you the best in the upcoming challenges. If you have any questions or need further assistance, please reach out to us.

Event Roadmap

Step 1: Registration - Complete your registration for Launchpad Kerala 2024.

Step 2: Confirmation - Look out for a confirmation email from the Launchpad Kerala 2024 team within 48 hours of registration. All updates will be posted on the updates page of the Launchpad Kerala 2024 website.

Step 3: Community Engagement - Join the Gtech MuLearn Discord Server and complete the Launchpad Challenges. Details here.

Step 4: Technical Challenge - Find tasks in the IEEE Launchpad category on the Discord channel. Submit tasks on time to earn Karma points.

Step 5: Aptitude Test - Participate in the online assessment to demonstrate your competencies to potential employers.

Step 6: Results - Your performance in the technical challenge and online test will be evaluated. Stay tuned for the results.

Step 7: Final Interview - The last stage of the selection process where you can showcase your skills and secure your desired job.
